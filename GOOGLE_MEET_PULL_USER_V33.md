# Google Meet Pull User Flow - V33

## What changed

Google Meet codes are no longer randomly generated by the app. Random codes cause Google's **Check your meeting code** screen.

The DM call flow now works like this:

1. Caller clicks the Google Meet button in DM.
2. App opens Google's official meeting creator: `https://meet.new`.
3. Caller copies the real Google-generated Meet URL.
4. App asks caller to paste that URL.
5. Backend validates the URL format.
6. App sends a Google Meet invite card into the DM.
7. Recipient receives a call notification and can click **Join Google Meet** from the DM card.

## Important limitation

Google does not allow a web app to create a valid Google Meet room and auto-pull another user without either:

- Google Calendar/Meet API OAuth, or
- a real Meet link generated by Google.

So this version avoids invalid fake codes and uses only real Google-generated Meet links.
